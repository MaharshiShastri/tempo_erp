Future ERP Modules and Roadmap
As the core CRM, Sales, and Logistics components mature, manufacturing ERPs generally scale outward into operations and fulfillment.

BOM (Bill of Materials) Engine: Link the items_master to a nested sub-assembly architecture. When a Sales Order is confirmed, the engine automatically calculates the raw materials required for the finished product.

Inventory Reorder Automation: Track stock levels based on GRN (Goods Receipt Notes) minus Sales Orders. Automatically trigger an internal Task for the Purchase Manager when raw materials hit a predefined minimum threshold.

Quality Control (QC) Sign-offs: Before an item reaches the Dispatch Planner, insert a QC module where Shop Floor Operators must electronically sign a checklist (using their JWT token) against the specific order_acceptance_id.
